Introduction:-
Mr.Bin is a website which helps you declutter your house and sell your waste of any kind. We further sell this waste to recyclers in bulk. Mr Bin reduces your burden to go to the local waste collection shops and sell your waste. Hence, helping in better waste management. 
Recyclers have to go to various scrap-yards to collect such waste, so our solution is to collect waste from all around i.e. offices, homes, factories, etc. by easily using our website and make it available for them all under one roof. Thereby reducing their effort of going in search of scrap-yards.
We work towards making the environment clean and green. So all the waste collected by us is recycled and put back in the world. We collect almost all categories of waste.

Aim Of Mr.Bin:-
Help you declutter your house as well as do good to the world by recycling and reusing items.

Scope of the website:-
Functionalities of Mr. Bin:-
•	Easily sell your waste with a couple of clicks.
•	Accurate measurement & proper pricing.
•	Recyclers can easily buy the amount of waste they want by entering the requirement.
•	Less time consuming & more organized.
Limitations:-
